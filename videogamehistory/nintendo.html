1966-toys

1985- US release of Nintendo entertainment system

Nintendo in Japan released the famicom, but they wanted to release it in the US, so they made it sound not like a console and took measures to make sure that games had higher quality,, including limiting how 3rd party developers could publish games for the console Shigero Miyamoto The creator of many of mintendo’s most popular franchises including mario and Zelda, which helped sell the nintendo entertainment system
