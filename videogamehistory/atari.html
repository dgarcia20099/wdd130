History of Atari

Atari is another popular video game company and while they may not have as long a history they are important in the history of video games

june 27th 1971 foundation of Atari

Atari was created by nolan nushnell and ted Dabney who had both previously worked on the first arcade cabinet formed Atari and released pong as an arcade game

1977 2600 releases

The first traditional game console released by Atari, had games released on cartidges

1975-first console release

The first console released by Atari was a home version of their popular game pong

1979- the foundation of activation

four atari employees after being tired of atari’s leadership left and formed there own company to develop games for the console

1982-atari 5200 released

The 5200 was the successor to the 2600 it was not a success and was discontinued in 1984

1983 video game crash

As a game developer at the time they had a part in the crash in 1983, but Atari had a particularly large part in it, with several low quality games released including the game E.T. Because some of these games sold poorly then Atari had the games buried in new Mexico Atari’s assets were sold by warner to tramel technology
